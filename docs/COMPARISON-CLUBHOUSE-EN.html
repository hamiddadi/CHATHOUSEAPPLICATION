Method & scoring

Each ChatHouse screen is compared to its Clubhouse counterpart and rated for parity: 100 = matches or exceeds Clubhouse for the intended function. Areas where ChatHouse exceeds Clubhouse are not penalised. The feature inventory comes from reading the real code (screen .tsx files + hooks/partials/services), not assumptions.

Strengths

  • Faithfully reproduces the Clubhouse core: scored hallway, live rooms (stage, raise hand + queue, full moderation, reactions, live captions, background audio), 3-level-privacy clubs with roles and approval, complete identity, phone onboarding in Clubhouse order, events + RSVP, Backchannel DM.
  • Exceeds Clubhouse on several axes: voice notes in DM, full group DMs, geo-social map, tips, premium, badges, full GDPR, language/category search filters.

Remaining gaps

  • Spatial audio ✕ and music mode / in-room sound sharing ✕ (Clubhouse signature features).
  • Replay depth: no scrub/clips/transcript.
  • No real waitlist / contacts sync at entry.
  • Polish: DM calls & attachments ("Coming soon"), online presence not wired, notification DM deep-link.
Conclusion. At ~88% screen parity, and given its extensions, ChatHouse is functionally on par with Clubhouse — and broader in feature coverage. It essentially only lacks three signature building blocks (spatial audio, music mode, rich replays) to reach near-total parity.
